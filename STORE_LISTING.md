## Full Description (max 4000 characters)
Color Dash is a fast-paced, reflex-testing mobile game where you guide a color-changing orb through an endless series of color gates.

**How to Play**
Swipe left and right to switch lanes. Your orb changes color after every gate — match your color to the gate's color and pass through the correct lane. Miss a gate or hit the wrong color, and it's game over!

**Features**
• Fast-paced endless gameplay with increasing difficulty
• 7 competitive leagues: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Master, and Champion
• Real-time global leaderboards — compete with players worldwide
• Promotion and relegation between leagues based on your best score
• Combo system that rewards consecutive correct gates
• Haptic feedback for an immersive experience
• Beautiful dark-themed UI with smooth animations
• Available in 12 languages: English, Arabic, German, Spanish, French, Hindi, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Russian, and Chinese
• Offline play supported — scores sync when you reconnect
• Privacy-first: no personal data collected, anonymous authentication only

**League System**
Start in Bronze and work your way up to Champion! Each league has a score threshold. Beat the threshold and get promoted. Your rank is displayed on the global leaderboard alongside players in the same league.

**Compete Globally**
See how you stack up against players around the world. The leaderboard updates in real time so you can watch your ranking climb as you improve.

Color Dash is free to play with no ads and no in-app purchases.
